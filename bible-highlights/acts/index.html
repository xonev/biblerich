“For we cannot but

speak

(like actors on a stage do)

the things which we have seen and heard . . . And with great power gave the apostles

witness (like actors on a stage do)

of the resurrection of the Lord Jesus” 4:20, 33, “

Go, stand and speak in the temple (like actors on a stage do)

to the people all the words of this life.” 5:20

Acts are performed on a stage and Actors perform on a stage, so a stage is used for the highlight of Acts.

(the left, top and right side lines can be made into the left, top, and right side lines of an A for Acts, and the bottom of the curtain line can be made into the line going across the middle of an A for Acts)

(to remember there are 28 chapters in Acts think of the 2 parts of a stage - 1. the curtain and 2. the floor, for the 2 in 28 chapters and the floor has been made with 8 sections for the 8 in 28 chapters in Acts)

7 Places of the New Testament Books

The next 9 books are written to people in 7 places, so a map with numbers in the order they appear in the Bible is given so you can easily locate and remember the highlights about each one.
