Nahum

the Elkoshite

. . .

affliction shall not

rise up

the second time.” 1:1, 9

You can think “high rise” and “rise up” are very similar in order to remember affliction not rising up the second time is in Nahum.

(the up and down lines of the high rise buildings of the city of Neneveh almost spell out Nahum with four of the letter, N-h-u-m, having up and down lines; and if you use a capital A for the A in Nahum it fits well in there, too)

(to remember there are 3 chapters in Nahum, the picture has 3 of the high rise buildings taller and skinnier than the others)
