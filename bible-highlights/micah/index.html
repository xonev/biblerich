(the left and right sides of the house can make the left and right side of an M for Micah then if you connect the top of the lines to the top of the door it will make the point for the middle point of the M for Micah and for Matthew which tells about the star the wise men saw over the house in Bethlehem)

(also, you can use the left side line of the house for the bottom of the i in Micah and the star can make the dot for the i in Micah)

(the top of the roof makes a leaning over 7 for 7 chapters in Micah, you can think of this perfect number 7 as the angel and God watching over the house where Jesus lived in Bethlehem)
