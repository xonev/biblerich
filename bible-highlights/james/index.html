James - Front End Loader

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

”faith with out works is dead” 2:20, “to him that knoweth to do good, and doeth it not, to him it is sin.” 4:17

Since these important and familiar verses are in James, a gold colored front end loader is used to represent working and doing. The gold color is used to indicate the works come from heavenly help.

(the curved front end scoop and arm on the left of the front end loader can be made into a J for James)

(to remember there are 5 chapters in James the top line of the engine of the front end loader can be made into the top line of the 5 in 5 chapters and the curved front scoop and arm on the left of the front end loader can be made into the line going down and the half circle of a 5 for 5 chapters in James)
