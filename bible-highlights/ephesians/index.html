In Revelation, Ephesus is one of the churches a letter is written to, so a church is used as the highlight of Ephesus. And since Ephesus is the 1st church in Revelation the number 1 with “st” is put on the top of the building.

Ephesus is on the east coast of the Mediterranean Sea so a church representing Ephesus is drawn on the right side of the sea.

Since “in heavenly places ” is mentioned 4 times & only in Ephesians, it was used as part of the highlight represented by the blue cloud and the 3 birds above the church. As it would follow, “spiritual wickedness in high places” is also in Ephesians.

Ephesians is the 5th of the 9 books written by Paul to a group of people in a place, Ephesus, with the book so named, so land is drawn.

(the left side line of the house along with the top and bottom line of the house can make the left, top, & bottom line of an E for Ephesians, and the top line of the door can make the middle line going across of an E for Ephesians)

(to remember there are 6 chapters in Ephesian the land going around the church can be made into a 6 for 6 chapters in Ephesians)
