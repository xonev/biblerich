Mark - Towel

{% include media.html %}

“The sabbath was

made for man,

and not man for the sabbath” 2:27

Mark is the 2nd of the 4 gospels and portrays Jesus as Servant, so a towel is used for the highlight of Mark because Jesus served the disciples by washing their feet and used a towel.

(the left side of the towel can be made into the left side of an M for Mark, the top and right line of the towel can be used to make the middle line of an M for Mark, and an additional line can be drawn to the right to make the right side of an M for Mark)

(to remember there are 16 chapters in Mark the left line of the towel can be made into a 1 for the 1 in 16 chapters and the right line of the towel with it curving at the bottom can be made into a 6)
