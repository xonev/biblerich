John

-

hands creating & giving to world

-

“All things were made

by him . . . For God so loved the

world

that he

gave

his only begotten Son” 1:3, 3:16

John is the 4th of the 4 gospels and portrays Jesus as God, so hands making & holding the world, and God giving the world his Son is used for the highlight of John.

(the left side of the hands can be made into a J for John, the circle of the world can be made into the “o” in John, the right hand fingers can be made into an “h” and an “n” in John)

(to remember there are 21 chapters in John the circle of the world can be made into the top circle shape of a 2 for 21 chapters and the right side line of the hands can be made into a 1 for the 1 in 21 chapters in John)
