Revelation - Little Jewel

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

“And the foundations of the wall of the city were garnished with all manner of precious stones.” 21:19

One little jewel is the highlight of Revelation, but all colors are used.

The last book of the Old Testament and the last book of the New Testament both end in jewel highlights & they both have the same number of chapters numbers in that Malachi has 4 chapters and Revelation has 22 chapters which can be thought of as 2+2=4. Malachi uses the big number of 4 to go with the big jewel and Revelation uses the little numbers of 2 for 22 to go with the little jewel.

(the left side line of the little jewel can be made into the left side of an R for Revelation the lines around the top of the little jewel can be made into the circle at the top of the R and the 2nd line going down on the the left side of the little jewel can be made into the bottom right line of an R for Revelation)

(there are 2 ways to remember there are 22 chapters in Revelation - 1. the left top and bottom lines of the little jewel can be made into a 2 and the right top and bottom lines can be made into a backwards 2 for 22 chapters in Revelation, and 2. the 2 areas at the bottom left of the little jewel and the 2 areas of the top and bottom right go together to make 2 and 2 for the 22 chapters in Revelation)
