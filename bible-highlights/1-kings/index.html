1 Kings - 2 Jewel Crown

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

“And Zadok the priest. . . anointed Solomon. . . and all the people said, God save king Solomon.” 1:39

(a “K” for kings can be drawn by making the left side of the “K” as the bottom of the crown and the 2 lines going out as the points of the top of the crown)

(1 Kings is about the kings from Solomon to Ahab, and the left side line of the crown can be made into a S for Solomon and the right side line of the crown can be made into an A for Ahab)

(to remember you are in the book of 1 Kings, 1 crown is drawn)

(since the first of 1 Kings, Adonijah tries to be king, you can make an A for Adonijah out of the lines of the 1st point on the crown, which makes 1st kings with a lot of A’s so it is easy to remember)

(to remember there are 22 chapters in 1 Kings, the left side of the crown is drawn in the shape of a 2 and the right side is drawn in the shape of a backwards 2 for 22 chapters)

(the 2 jewels also represent the 22 chapters)

(the 2 jewels change depending on what color the major king or kings are of that chapter)
