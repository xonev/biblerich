“Moses stretched out his hand over the sea . . . and the waters were divided.” 14:21

(the two lines of the waves can make the top and bottom lines of an E for Exodus and the bottom of the sea line would be the left side of the E for Exodus, and then imagine the people going through the Red Sea to make the middle line of the E for Exodus)

(the two lines of the waves can also make the two sides of a 4 for 40 chapters in Exodus)

(also the X in Exodus you can think of as a sideways 4, and the O in Exodus for the 0 in the 40 for 40 chapters in Exodus)
