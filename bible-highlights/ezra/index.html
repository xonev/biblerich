“Now this is the copy of the

letter

that the king Artaxerxes gave unto

Ezra

. . . that all they . . . which are minded of their own free will to go up to Jerusalem, go with thee . . . And thou,

Ezra

, after the wisdom of thy God,

that is in thine hand

, set magistrates and judges, which may judge all the people that are beyond the river, all such as know the laws of thy God; and teach ye they that know them not.” 7:11, 13, 23

Many letters were written in Ezra, especially this one in chapter 7 from Artaxerxes, to restore Jerusalem, marking the beginning of the 2300 days, so an envelope is used with a hand holding it for the highlight of Ezra)

(the left line of the envelope and the top & bottom line of the envelope make the side and top & bottom line on the letter E for Ezra)

(to remember there are 10 chapters in Ezra think of the left side of the envelope for the 1 and the circle of the hand for the 0)
