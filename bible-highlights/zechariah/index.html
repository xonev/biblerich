Zechariah - Heavenly Temple & Streets Of Gold

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

“In the eighth month, in the second year of Darius, came the word of the LORD unto Zechariah, the son of Berechiah” 1:1, “the hands of Zerubbabel have laid the foundation of this house; his hands shall also finish it” 4:9, “Behold the man whose name is The BRANCH . . . he shall build the temple of the LORD“ 6:12, “and the streets of the city shall be full of boys and girls playing in the streets thereof” 8:5, “Rejoice greatly. . . Jerusalem: behold, thy King cometh . . . lowly and riding upon . . . a colt” 9:9, “so they weighed for my price thirty pieces of silver (cast down in temple)” 11:12, “What are these wounds in thine hands? Then he shall answer, Those with which I was wounded in the house of my friends” 13:6

Haggai and Zechariah are the 2 prophets who encouraged Zerubbabel and Joshua to continue rebuilding the temple and city of Jerusalem, they are even mentioned in Ezra 5:1.

Zechariah is full of prophecies of what would happen to Jesus at the earthly temple and in the heavenly city, so a temple set in heaven - colored gold and drawn with a cloud - with a gold street is drawn so you can imagine all these things happening there.

(the left side of the street squares can be made into a Z and an E for Zechariah, and the left line of the cloud can be made into a C for the C in Zechariah, so you can remember this book name has the Zec for Zechariah as opposed to the book Zephaniah with a Zep)

(you can also think of the “c” for “clouds” for the temple that is in the “clouds” as opposed to the earthly temple for Haggai)

(to remember Zechariah has 14 chapters in it the right line of the heavenly temple can be made into a 1 for the 1 in the 14 chapters and the street squares on the right can be made into a 4 for the 4 in the 14 chapters in Zechariah)

(also to remember Zechariah has 14 chapters, if you think there are 2 prophets - 1. Haggai and 2. Zechariah that encourage the 2 leaders - 1. Zerubbabel and 2. Joshua to rebuild the temple and 2 prophets + 2 leaders = 4 men, then Haggai with 2 chapters after that would be Zechariah with a 4 in the number of its chapters)
