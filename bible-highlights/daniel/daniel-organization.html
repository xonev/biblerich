Daniel Organization

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

Daniel is organized for the most part in 2 categories - stories and prophecies (to the end of time). The first half of the book, chapters 1-6, are stories and the last half of the book, chapters 7-12, are prophecies.

Chapter 2 falls in both the story and the prophecy category.

Notice that the chapters are not in time order. Daniel receives the time prophecies of chapters 7 and 8 before chapters 5 and 6.

Chapters 6 (the lion’s den) and 9 (Daniel’s prayer and prophecy of the Messiah) happened the same year.

Daniel was very young, about twenty years old when he received the first prophecy in chapter 2 and very old, about 90, when he received the majority of the time prophecies.
