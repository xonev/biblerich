“And the rest of the acts of Hezekiah . . . are they not written in the book of the chronicles of the kings of Judah?” 2 Kings 20:20

(1 Chronicles is about the generations from Adam to king David, to help you remember this the 2 on the left can be made into a cursive A for Adam and the top of the 9 can be made into a D for David)

(1 roll scroll is drawn so you know you are in 1 Chronicles)

(to remember there are 29 chapters in 1 Chronicles, the ends of the scroll are made into a 2 and a 9, and the left side of the 9 can be thought of as the shape of a “C” for Chronicles)
