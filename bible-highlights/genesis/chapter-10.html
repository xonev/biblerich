Genesis 10

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

Chapters 5 & 10 have the big blocks of generations in them. If you think of your hands, one has 5 fingers and together they have 10, you can remember these chapters like you are counting the people or generations.

For easy remembrance think these chapters as chapter 5 has the first of the alphabet from A-N (for Adam to Noah) and Chapter 10 has the middle of the alphabet from N-P (from Noah to Peleg). Also alphabetically, “n” for Noah comes a little before “p” for Peleg when you say, generations from Noah to Peleg, for in his days was the earth divided.

Peleg means division. He was named this as a prophecy that the earth would divide during his lifetime and no longer be Pangaea, one land mass. You can think of the big 1 as a knife to divide the earth to help you remember this is the chapter where the earth was divided.

The big 0 is made into the divided earth. The water part of the earth can make you think of Noah in the flood, like in Genesis 5.

The little 10 on the bottom right is put together to make a bow and arrow illustrating Nimrod being a mighty hunter.

The book highlight is heaven & earth so they are drawn in the big 0 because this is what the earth looked like in this chapter.
