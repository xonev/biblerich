Genesis 8

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

In the little 8 at the bottom left, the bubbles are drawn coming off the earth making an 8 shape illustrating the waters abating. If you think of the rhyme, waters abate in chapter 8, it will help you associate them together.

The two circles of the big 8 are made into the two birds that Noah sent out. The raven 1st, on the top and the dove last, on the bottom.

The book highlight is heaven & earth so they are drawn as the earth with bubbles because this is what was happening to the earth in this chapter.
