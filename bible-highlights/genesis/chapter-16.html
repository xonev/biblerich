Hagar was younger than Sarai, to have a child, so you can think 16 is a young age to remember Hagar having a son for them.

Hagar bears Ishmael by Abram. This is shown by the bottom of the big 6 being drawn into a baby wrapped in blue for a son. With the baby at the bottom of the six can make you think that this was a low, incorrect way to make God’s promise come true.

The big 1 is made into the words 1st to represent Abram’s first son.

This idea was man’s way and man was created on day 6 also for the 6 in 16. The book highlight is heaven & earth so the earth is placed under the 6 because God created man & the earth.
