Genesis 18

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

A 3 can be made on the right side of an 8, for the 3 strangers visit.

The big 1 is drawn into the Lord coming to Abraham. He is standing tall like a 1.

They ate is represented by the top circle of the big eight made into a plate of food. Ate sounds like 8 for the 8 in chapter 18.

The conversation to the left is what the visit was about and the “too” in “Is anything too hard for the Lord” can be an 18 - the t for the 1 and the oo for the 8 (2 o’s on top of each other).

This chapter is the 2nd of at least 5 Genesis chapters that prophesy that Jesus (the line of these patriarchs) would bless all people of the earth. The highlight of the heaven & earth is drawn with a little 1, for chapter 18, made into a man to represent in Abraham, or you can think of the little man as being Jesus, and the little 8 for chapter 18 is made into hands over the earth blessing all the nations of the earth.

The O on the lower part of the big 8 represents the numbers that Abram asked the Lord not to destroy Sodom by - 50, 40, (45), 30, 20, 10 - pretty much by 10’s.

The book highlight is heaven & earth so they are drawn under the phrase ‘is anything too hard for the Lord’ because God made heaven & earth which was not too hard for God.
