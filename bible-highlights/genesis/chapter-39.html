Genesis 39

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

Turn the 3 on its side and it becomes a “w,” which is for wife; turn the 9 backwards and it becomes a “P” for Potiphar; so you can have 39 stand for Wife of Potiphar.

The big 3 is made into the hands of Potiphar’s wife trying to grab Joseph.

In the bottom of the big 9 Joseph is running away as she got his garment, which is made out of the top of the big 9.

Wickedness also starts with a “w” which turned can be a 3 so you can easily put the phrase “How then can I do this great wickedness and sin against God” in chapter 39 with the 2 “w”s for wife and wickedness.

The heaven & earth are placed by Potiphar’s wife because she was reaching for worldly, earthy lusts and passions.
