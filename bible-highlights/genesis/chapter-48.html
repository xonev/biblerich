Genesis 48

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

The big 4 is Pharaoh’s throne and the heaven & earth placed under it because he was the major ruler of the earth.

There are two heads made from the 2 circles of the big 8 representing Manasseh and Ephraim, Joseph’s sons. Jacob’s arms are crossed as he is blessing them opposite (making a figure 8). If you cross your arms and touch your elbows with your fingertips it will also make an 8. This is an easy way to remember chapter 48.
