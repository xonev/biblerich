Genesis 15

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

There are 5 things that happen in this chapter that start with the letter ‘S’; and 5 & S have the same shape, so you can relate it to a 5 star hotel.

You can think of this chapter as a treasure map where you stay in a 5 star hotel on your trip, you know the stars are shining at night because you are outside looking for the treasure and you need a lamp.

The stars on the top of the big 5 represent the Seed as Stars promise to Abram.

Then the line of the left side of the big 5 represents Israel going down to Egypt and being

Strangers and afflicted 400 years (think of chapter 15 as 5 -1 = 4 to remember the 400 years). Then the half circle of the big 5 is they would go out of Egypt with great Substance, which is represented by jewels and gold. You can think of a 5 as the same shape of $ (a dollar sign) to easily remember this is the chapter where the Egyptians are prophesied to give (money) great substance to the Israelites when God delivers them.

The big 1 is drawn as a lamp with smoke coming out of it to represent the Burning Lamp and Smoking Furnace covenant, with 5 animals (you can also think of this for the 5 in 15).

The book highlight is heaven & earth so they are placed by the ‘seed as stars’ because Abram would have many descendants on the earth.
