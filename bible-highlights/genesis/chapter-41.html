Genesis 41

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

Again the big 4 is Pharaoh’s throne and the heaven & earth are placed under it because he was the major ruler of the earth.

The big 1 is made into a skinny corn stalk & ears that are withered away and could not get fat due to the 7 years of famine in Pharaoh’s dream. You can also think of the top of the 1 as a little 7 for the 7 years of plenty and 7 years of famine.

Even though it is not drawn, you can also think that the 1 could be a skinny cow, unlike the plump baker of 40.

Joseph’s wife’s name was Asenath (begins with an “A” - first letter of the alphabet). The name Pharaoh gave Joseph was Zaphnath-paaneah (begins with a “Z” - last letter of the alphabet). This makes their names easy to remember.

Joseph only has 2 sons and they were born in this chapter - Manasseh and Ephraim. The oldest was Manasseh and youngest was Ephraim. Their initials spell out the word ME, so you can easily remember who was older and who was younger.

Joseph now has a family of 4 in this chapter instead of just himself, 1 alone in Egypt, for chapter 41.
