Genesis 19

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

In the top left little 19, the 1 is made into a man and the 9 is made into a shut eye to represent the 2 angels making the men of Sodom blind.

The big 1 is the fire and the big 9 is the brimstone (the balls of burning sulphur in the shape of a 9) raining down on Sodom and Gomorrah. Using the numbers of this chapter (1 and 9), for 911, you can think, this was a 911 burning disaster on the earth at that time.

In the little 19 on the bottom left, the little 9 is made into an eye to represent Lot’s wife looking back with her eyes at the city and the 1 is made into a light blue, clear looking lady to represent Lot’s wife turning into a pillar of salt.

In the little 19 at the top right, The 1 is made into a man to represent Lot getting drunk and the 9 made into a woman with child is drawn to represent Lot’s daughters that he sleeps with.

Wine and 9 rhyme. You can remember the 2 wine incidences of Genesis because they are in chapters 9 and 19 with the 9’s that rhyme with wine.

The daughters bare Moab and Benammi, which became the Moabites and the Ammonites.

The heaven & earth are placed by the ‘911’ because this was a 911 burning disaster at this time on the earth.
