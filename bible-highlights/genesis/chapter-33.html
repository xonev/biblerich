Genesis 33

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

A 3 backwards looks like an E. The first letters of Esau Embraces flipped around could then be 33.

The first big 3 are lips representing Esau kissing Jacob.

The second big 3 is Esau’s arms outstretched to embrace Jacob.

In this chapter, Esau goes to Seir and Jacob goes to Shechem (in the land of Canaan), spreads his tent there, and buys a field for 100 pieces of money. You can also think of these hands as paying money for this land.

The heaven & earth are placed by Jacob’s name because the earth would be blessed through his line.
