Genesis 36

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

The big 3 you can again think of as a backwards “E” for Esau (&/or Edom).

The big 6 you can think of as a backwards ”d” for descendants.

The 3 & 6 are colored red for Esau’s red hair.

The line at the 6 is going down or “descend”ing (for descendants) to his family circle containing familiar names such as Eliphaz & Teman (mentioned in Job). He had dukes (so they were a type of royalty) and they were also called Edomites because Esau was Edom. The heaven & earth are placed in the word ‘descendants’ because they were Esau’s descendant upon the earth.
