Genesis 27

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

The big 2 is made into Rebekah putting the kid’s goat skin on Jacob’s hands and neck.

Jacob is drawn into the big 7 with one arm stretched out to make a 7. Rebekah wanted Jacob to be like Esau with the hair so that he can deceive Isaac for the blessing.

Esau and Jacob were twins and “Twin deceiving” and “twenty seven” sound very similar, which can be another clue to help you remember chapter 27 is about a twin deceiving his father.

The heaven & earth are placed by the word ‘blessing’ to be a blessing on the earth.
