Genesis 14

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

Think of Abram rescuing Lot with a lasso made out of the big 4.

The big 1 is made into Melchizedek king of Salem, which means peace, standing tall with his hands out to bless Abram. His body and hands out make the beginning of the shape of a big 4 also. If Abram was standing there being blessed in front of Melchizedek, it would make a complete 4.

The bottom right is a little 1 made into a man to represent Abram and there is a little 4 made out of Abrams arm’s and the big bag he is holding to represent Abram giving the tithe of all to Mechizedek.

The book highlight is heaven & earth so they are drawn under ‘Melchizedek blesses Abram’ because he blessed him on this earth.
