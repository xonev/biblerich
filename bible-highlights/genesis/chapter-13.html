Genesis 13

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

The big 3 is drawn like a map to show how Abram and Lot separate. From the middle of the big 3, Abram goes to Hebron and Lot goes east to Sodom.

The top of the big 1 goes out in the 4 directions of N,E,S,W like a compass to help illustrate look northward, southward, eastward, and westward - all the land which thou seest, to thee will I give it promise God gave to Abram after the separation.

At the bottom of the big 1 is dust to illustrate I will make thy seed as the dust of the earth promise He also gave him.

The book highlight is heaven & earth so the heaven & earth are placed by the N,E,S,W to show the land of the earth God promised Abram.
