Genesis 21

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

The top of the big 2 is Isaac born as a baby, that this was the high, correct way to have the promised son. This is also represented by the arrow beside the big 1 going up.

The bottom is Hagar and Ishmael cast out of the camp into the world for mocking Isaac, because this was a lowly thing to do. This is also represented by the arrow by the big 1 going down for being cast out.

The big 1 represents the letter “I” for the first letter of the 2 sons, Isaac and Ishmael. So you can think of 21 as the 2 “I” names of Abraham’s sons. The book highlight is heaven & earth so they are drawn under the big 2 where Hagar and Ishmael are being cast out into the world.
