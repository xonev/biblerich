Genesis 17

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

If you put the 1 and 7 right next to each other they kind of look like the letter “n” for names and nations.

Abram and Sarai are drawn at the top of the big 7 because God gave them new names, Abraham and Sarah.

He said they would be the father and mother of many nations, which is what the new names mean, so nations are written under them going down the big 7 to represent the generations of nations after them. They were given this promise at ages 99 & 90 and Abraham laughed.

The big 1 is a knife because here was where the circumcision covenant started. If you add 17, 1 + 7 = 8, 8 is how many days old the sons had to be for circumcision.

The book highlight is heaven & earth so they are placed by the ‘nations’ because there are nations on the earth.
