Genesis 23

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

Once again Sarah is made into the big 2 with her skirt flowing out at the bottom of the 2. The backwards curve of a 2 is in the shape of an S, so you can also associate Sarah, shekel, and silver together for this chapter.

The heart beat symbol at the bottom left, shows that she died because it stops as it hits the bottom of the big 2 (called a flat line for no heart beat or death). You can also see that she died at 127 years old (the 2 is the big 2 with the little 1 on the left and the little 7 on the right of the big 2).

The top of the big 3 is made into the cave that Abraham buys and Sarah is buried in. The top of the big 3 also makes a backwards “c” for cave.

The bottom is a map so you can see where Hebron is (where the cave is) in the land of Canaan. The bottom of the 3 also makes a backwards “c” for Canaan. So you can think of the 3 as 2 backwards “c’s” for cave in Canaan.

23 is in numerical order, so counting up 2, 3, 4, it can represent the 4 in 400 shekels of silver that Abram bought the cave for.

The burial cave in Canaan is on the earth, so the heaven & earth are placed by ‘cave in Canaan.’
