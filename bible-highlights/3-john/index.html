3 John - 3 Curved Hearts

”unto the well beloved Gaius, whom I

love

in the truth.” 1:1

Since John is the beloved disciple and John speaks about love so much in his writings, 3 curved hearts with eyes, arms and legs are used as the highlight for 3 John.

(the curved left or right side of the hearts can be made into upside down J’s for John)

(there are two ways to remember there is 1 chapter in 3 John - 1. The arms of the hearts have arms going out that can be connected to make 1’s and look like they are holding 1’s for 1 chapter in 3 John, and 2. 2 John and 3 John have the numbers 2 and 3, if you add them together 2+3=5, they are so loving they go together and share their numbers with 1 John who only has 1 number with his name and 1 John shares his number with 2 John and 3 John and they get a 1 for 1 chapter in 2 John and 1 chapter in 3 John)
