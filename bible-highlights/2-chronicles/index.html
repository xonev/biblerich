2 Chronicles

-

2 roll scroll with 36

- “ And the rest of the acts of Hezekiah . . . are they not

written in the book of the chronicles

of the kings of Judah?” 2 Kings 20:20

(2 Chronicles is about the generations from Solomon to Zedekiah, to help you remember this the 3 on the left can be made into an S for Solomon and the top of the 6 can be made into a Z for Zedekiah)

(the Chronicles’ kings then look like this: 1 Chronicles A-D, 2 Chronicles S-Z, which are in Alphabetical Order)

(2 roll scroll is drawn so you know you are in 2 Chronicles)

(to remember there are 36 chapters in 2 Chronicles, the ends of the scroll are made into a 3 and a 6,

and the left side of the 6 can be thought of as the shape of a “C” for Chronicles

)
