Luke - L U K E Man

{% include picture-page-header.html %} {% include media.html %}

“And she brought forth her first born son, and wrapped him in swaddling clothes, and laid him in a manager.” 2:7, “for a spirit hath not flesh and bones, as ye see me have.” 24:39

Luke is the 3rd of the 4 gospels and portrays Jesus as a Man. From birth to life after death, Jesus still has a body of a man as Luke tells us. A man is used for the highlight for Mark made out of the letters L-u-k-e.

(the nose of the man makes the L in Luke, the bottom of the face makes the “u” in Luke, the body and legs makes the “k” in Luke, and the hand going out makes the “e” in Luke)

(to remember there are 24 chapters in Luke the nose in the L shape can be the left line of a 4 and the line going across as the 4 and the shoulder and leg of the man can be made into a 2 for 24 chapters in Luke)
