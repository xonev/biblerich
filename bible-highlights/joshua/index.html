Joshua - Fallen Wall With Scarlet Thread

“Joshua said unto the people, Shout; for the LORD hast given you the city. . . So . . . the people shouted with a great shout, that the wall fell down flat” 6:16, 20

Chapter 6 is where the wall falls down flat, so the wall is drawn flat to make the bottom of a 6 and the scarlet thread that saved Rahab is drawn to make the top of the 6.

(the scarlet thread is made into a J for Joshua)

(the scarlet thread can look like a 2 for the 2 in the 24 chapters of Joshua and the outside walls as the two side lines of a 4 in 24 chapters)
