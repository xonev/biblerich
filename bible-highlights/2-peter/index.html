2 Peter

-

2 stones

-”Simon, whom he also named

Peter

” Luke 6:14

Since Jesus renamed Simon to Peter, which means a stone, 2 dark green stones are used for the highlight for 2 Peter.

(the circle of the stone on the left can make the top circle of a P for Peter and the circle of the stone on the right can be made into the circle of a lower case “e” for the “e’s” in Peter)

(to remember there are 3 chapters in 2 Peter the curved tops of the stones can make a sideways 3 for 3 chapters in 2 Peter)
